About Q-ENCORE

Q-ENCORE is Quantum University's premier music club, dedicated to fostering musical talent and creating unforgettable experiences. From live performances to workshops and jam sessions, we provide a platform for students to explore and express their passion for music.

Founded in 2020, our club has grown to become a vibrant community of musicians, vocalists, and music enthusiasts from all backgrounds and skill levels.

What We Offer

  • Regular jam sessions
  • Music workshops
  • Open mic nights
  • Instrument training
  • Annual music festival
